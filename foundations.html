Project Foundations

For the successful development and use of the Student Digital Planner, several key assumptions guide this project:

  • Access to Technology: Students will have access to smartphones, tablets, or computers to use the digital planner.
  • Student Engagement: Students will be willing to adopt new productivity tools to improve their time management.
  • Project Timeline: The development process can be completed within the planned timeframe for testing and feedback.
  • Institutional Support: Schools and administrators may support the distribution and use of the planner.
  • Resource Availability: Free platforms such as Google Sheets and Canva will remain accessible during the project.
