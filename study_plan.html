Level 1 – Beginner High School Study Plan

Objectives: Build basic study habits, track homework, improve organization.

Weekly Structure:

  • Sunday: Plan week, list assignments, set 3 goals.
  • Monday-Friday: Review notes, complete homework, update planner.
  • Saturday: Review subjects, prep for next week.

Level 2 – Intermediate High School Study Plan

Objectives: Balance academics with extracurriculars, improve productivity.

  • 4:00–4:30 PM: Review notes.
  • 4:30–5:30 PM: Homework.
  • 6:00–6:45 PM: Test prep.
  • 7:30–8:00 PM: Planner update and prep for next day.

Level 3 – Advanced High School Study Plan

Objectives: Exam prep, practice questions, progress tracking.

  • 4:00–5:00 PM: Homework.
  • 5:00–5:30 PM: Break.
  • 5:30–6:30 PM: Study/practice questions.
  • 8:00–8:15 PM: Update planner and review goals.
  • Weekend: Past exam questions and weekly evaluation.

Level 1 – Beginner Tertiary Study Plan

Objectives: Transition to independent study, build organization, track assignments.

  • Sunday: List lectures, assignments, readings, set goals.
  • Daily: Review notes, complete readings/assignments, update planner.

Level 2 – Intermediate Tertiary Study Plan

Objectives: Balance coursework with jobs/extracurriculars, prioritize deadlines.

  • Morning: Attend lectures.
  • Afternoon: Review notes, complete readings.
  • Evening: Assignments/projects.
  • Weekly review: Evaluate progress, adjust schedule.

Level 3 – Advanced Tertiary Study Plan

Objectives: Manage complex workloads, research, internships, high academic performance.

  • 8:00–12:00: Lectures or research.
  • 1:00–3:00: Assignments/projects.
  • 3:00–4:00: Break.
  • 4:00–6:00: Study or project work.
  • Weekend: Review progress, plan next week, update planner.
