FAQ

1. What problem does this project address?

Many students struggle with time management and organization, which negatively affects their academic performance and productivity.


2. Why is time management important for students?

Effective time management helps students complete assignments on time, reduce stress, and improve overall academic success.


3. What challenges do students commonly face?

Students often face missed deadlines, incomplete assignments, and difficulty balancing schoolwork with other responsibilities.


4. Who is affected by poor time management?

This issue affects students who must balance coursework, extracurricular activities, and sometimes part-time jobs.


5. What is the proposed solution?

Our group proposes a Student Digital Planner and Productivity Toolkit to help students manage their time more effectively.


6. What is a Student Digital Planner?

A digital planner is an online tool that helps students organize tasks, assignments, schedules, and goals.


7. What features will the planner include?

The planner will include weekly goal-setting sheets, assignment tracking tools, reminders, and structured planning templates.


8. What platforms will be used?

The planner will be designed using accessible platforms such as Google Sheets and Canva.


9. Why were these platforms chosen?

They are free, easy to use, and widely accessible to students.


10. What makes this solution innovative?

The planner focuses on simplicity and accessibility rather than complex productivity features.


11. How is it different from other productivity tools?

It is customizable, simple to use, and specifically designed for student needs.


12. How will the planner help students stay organized?

Students can track deadlines, plan study sessions, and monitor assignment progress.


13. What are the expected outcomes?

Improved organization, better time management, reduced stress, and improved academic performance.


14. How will students benefit?

They will gain better control over their schedules and academic responsibilities.


15. What technology access is assumed?

We assume students have access to smartphones, tablets, or computers.


16. What if students lack technology access?

Accessibility may become limited and reduce the project's effectiveness.


17. What assumption is made about student participation?

The project assumes students are willing to adopt productivity tools.


18. Why is student engagement important?

Without engagement, the planner cannot effectively improve time management.


19. What role does institutional support play?

School administrators may help support and distribute the planner.


20. What resources are required?

The project relies on free digital platforms remaining accessible.
